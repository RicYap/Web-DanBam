    About Us

    DanBam is a traditional korean restaurant that serves korean food as its main dish. Established since 2021, DanBam presents authentic korean flavors because almost all the raw materials used are imported directly from Korea.     History of DanBam Restaurant

    The beginning of DanBam when two brothers, Daniel Sugito and Bram Sugito thought about the money given by his father to open a restaurant. Then they both think that the restaurant that is booming right now is a Korean restaurant. Before becoming DanBam Restaurant, this restaurant had the name Sugito Restaurant. However, after 1 year of opening a restaurant, the resto is always quiet and not crowded. Then they both thought to remodel the name and menu in the restaurant.

    After a few weeks, DanBam Restaurant became a replacement name of suginto restaurant which is considered incompatible with traditional Korean resto. DanBam is taken from the first name Daniel and Bram. Not only the name of the restaurant is changed but also many menus are added, ranging from barbeque, dessert, etc. After that change, DanBam developed and many people knew DanBam Korean Resto. Daniel and Bram also opened branches in several locations and all had above average reviews and ratings.
